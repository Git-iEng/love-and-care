About Us

At Love and Care Foundation, we believe that compassion is the strongest force for change. Founded with a vision to uplift underprivileged individuals and communities, our mission is to create a world where no one feels abandoned or forgotten. We are a registered non-profit organisation based in Pune, Maharashtra, working on a no-profit, no-loss basis, dedicated to social welfare, education, health, women empowerment, and community development.

What began as a simple desire to help has now evolved into a growing initiative where young and passionate individuals come together to serve the elderly, children, women, and differently-abled with dignity and care. We aim to address the social, educational, and emotional needs of those who are often left behind.

From supporting orphanages and old-age homes to organizing food donation drives and awareness programs, Love and Care Foundation is committed to making a lasting impact through every small action that matters.

Our Objectives

Our organisation is guided by a wide range of objectives aimed at the holistic development of individuals and society. Below are some of our core objectives:

  • To provide education, training, rehabilitation, and emotional care to all individuals in need, including orphans, widows, differently-abled persons, and the elderly.
  • To support underprivileged children by offering learning materials, school supplies, and basic necessities.
  • To promote health awareness, establish diagnostic centres, and support individuals affected by AIDS, addiction, or natural disasters.
  • To empower women through skill development, reproductive health education, and rights awareness.
  • To run educational institutions offering professional, technical, and vocational courses such as engineering, law, management, agriculture, and the arts.
  • To start elder care programs, youth development programs, and awareness seminars on education and employment.
  • To build homes and hostels for children, women, elderly, and differently-abled individuals.
  • To work alongside government and NGO partners for holistic health, education, employment, and environmental development.
  • To support and rehabilitate individuals affected by natural calamities like floods, earthquakes, and famine.

Educational Empowerment

To provide education, training, and rehabilitation support for underprivileged children, women, and differently-abled individuals. To distribute free educational materials such as books, notebooks, and stationery to economically disadvantaged students. To establish and operate educational institutions, training centers, and vocational programs offering courses in Arts, Science, Commerce, and Professional disciplines, with a special focus on rural and marginalized communities.

Moreover, the club seeks to establish and operate schools, training centers, and vocational institutes offering comprehensive courses in Arts, Science, Commerce, and various professional fields. Special emphasis is placed on reaching rural and marginalized communities, with the goal of expanding access to quality education and equipping individuals with the knowledge and skills necessary for personal development and societal contribution.

Women and Girl Child Welfare

The Love and Care organisation is deeply committed to the empowerment and protection of women and girl children, recognizing their vital role in the development of a just and inclusive society. Through structured awareness programs, the organization educates communities on gender equality, women’s rights, personal health, hygiene, and safety, aiming to dismantle social stigma and promote dignity for all women.

Special attention is given to supporting female school dropouts by facilitating their re-admission into the education system and equipping them with skill development opportunities that lead to economic independence.

Additionally, the Club prioritizes health and wellness by promoting reproductive health awareness, conducting menstrual hygiene sessions, and ensuring access to basic gynecological care, thereby fostering the overall well-being and self-reliance of women and girls from underserved backgrounds.

Healthcare and Medical Services

The organization conducts regular health awareness programs and free medical check-up camps to promote early detection and prevention of common illnesses, especially in underserved communities. It also provides essential diagnostic and first-aid facilities to address immediate health concerns.

To strengthen its healthcare outreach, the club aims to establish medical support units such as pathology labs, X-ray centers, and community health education hubs. In addition, it works to raise awareness and offer support for individuals affected by chronic illnesses, HIV/AIDS, substance abuse, and alcoholism, promoting rehabilitation and social reintegration through counseling, treatment access, and community-based care initiatives.

Social Welfare and Relief Activities

The Love and Care Social Club is committed to social welfare and humanitarian relief, focusing on the care and protection of society’s most vulnerable groups. The organization aims to provide safe shelter, nourishment, and emotional support through the establishment of hostels and care homes for orphans, destitute women, the elderly, persons with disabilities, and other marginalized individuals.

In times of natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, and pandemics, the club actively engages in emergency relief and rehabilitation efforts by distributing food, clothing, medicine, and other essential supplies. Furthermore, it undertakes a range of charitable initiatives including community kitchens, food drives, and livelihood support programs to uplift economically disadvantaged families, ensuring their basic needs are met with compassion and dignity.

Skill Development and Livelihood Support

As part of its commitment to self-reliance and dignity of labor, the Love and Care aims to: Provide Skill-Based Training: Facilitate free or subsidized skill development programs in crafts, tailoring, computer literacy, agriculture, and other locally relevant trades to empower youth, women, and marginalized individuals.

Promote Livelihood Opportunities: Assist in identifying sustainable livelihood avenues, especially in rural and semi-urban areas, through training, awareness, and linkage with self-help groups (SHGs) and micro-enterprises.
