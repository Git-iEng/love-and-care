Visit to Apala Ghar Orphanage & Old Age Home

As our first heartfelt initiative, the Love and Care team embarked on a meaningful visit to Apala Ghar, a nurturing space that serves both as an orphanage for children and a home for elderly individuals. With around 40 residents of all ages, Apala Ghar is more than just a shelter — it’s a home filled with hope, stories, and resilience.

Upon our arrival, we were welcomed with warm smiles and open hearts. We spent quality time engaging in conversations with both the children and the senior residents, learning about their lives, their dreams, and the challenges they’ve faced. As part of our support, we served freshly prepared meals, shared laughter, and offered companionship — a simple act that brought immense joy to everyone present.

We were touched to see the holistic environment that Apala Ghar provides. The centre boasts a small in-house hospital for medical needs, creative and recreational spaces for children, including indoor and outdoor play areas, and structured music instrument coaching classes that encourage artistic expression. The elderly have access to a serene garden space where they can find peace, reflect, and spend time in nature.

What stood out the most was the bond between generations living under one roof — children finding wisdom in the elderly, and seniors finding joy in the innocence of youth. This harmonious setting reflects the very values that Love and Care stands for: Every Helping Hand Creates a Brighter Tomorrow.

Our visit to Apala Ghar was a humbling experience — one that reminded us of the impact even a single act of kindness can make. It not only strengthened our resolve to continue this journey but also gave us a deeper understanding of the communities we aim to serve. This project remains a proud milestone, marking the beginning of our mission to build a more empathetic and inclusive society.
