Welcome to Love and Care

Every Helping Hand Creates a Brighter Tomorrow

At Love and Care Foundation, we believe in the power of small acts of kindness to create big change. Our journey began with a simple desire — to reach out and support those who are often overlooked, including underprivileged children, the elderly, and individuals in need. With love as our foundation and care as our mission, we are here to uplift lives and bring hope where it’s needed most.

We are a team of passionate changemakers, working on a no-profit, no-loss basis to serve communities through education, healthcare, and humanitarian aid. Whether it's feeding the hungry, supporting orphanages, or empowering women through learning and self-reliance, every initiative is a step toward building a kinder, more inclusive society.

Our projects are designed to create lasting impact. From educational support for children to rehabilitation programs for the elderly and differently-abled, we work tirelessly to ensure that every individual we reach feels valued, supported, and inspired to grow. We also collaborate with other organizations and government bodies to amplify our efforts and create sustainable solutions.

Change begins with care — and you can be a part of it. Join us as a volunteer, donor, or supporter, and help us write stories of hope, courage, and transformation. At Love and Care Foundation, every helping hand adds meaning to our mission. Together, let’s spread love, share smiles, and build a future where everyone has a chance to thrive.

Our Projects

Visit to Apala Ghar Orphanage & Old Age Home

As our first step in spreading kindness, the Love and Care team visited Apala Ghar, a combined orphanage and old age home that houses around 40 residents, including children and elderly individuals. During our visit, we provided meals, shared heartfelt conversations, and explored the well-maintained facility, which includes a small hospital, activity areas, indoor and outdoor play zones, music instrument coaching, and a peaceful garden for the elderly. The experience was both touching and inspiring, marking the beginning of our journey toward building a more compassionate and inclusive society.

Our Events

Opening Ceremony – 8th March 2025

The Love and Care Foundation was officially launched on 8th March 2025, a date that coincides with International Women’s Day — a celebration of compassion, strength, and unity. The occasion marked the beginning of our mission to serve communities with empathy and commitment. The event featured a symbolic ribbon-cutting ceremony, reflections on our shared vision, and meaningful interactions among our team and supporters. It was more than just a celebration—it was a heartfelt promise to uplift lives and spread kindness. Inspired by the incredible women who lead and support us, the foundation begins its journey with purpose, unity, and a deep sense of social responsibility.

About us

  • Feed the Hungry Child

    Love and Care runs the Feed the Hungry Child program to provide food to children who do not get regular meals. We focus on areas where children face hunger due to poverty or lack of support. With the help of donations and volunteers, we prepare and distribute meals to those in need.

    We also give food kits to families who are unable to afford basic groceries. The aim is to reduce hunger among children and help them stay healthy. This program is one of the steps we take to support underprivileged communities.

    Read More

  • Brightening Futures Through Education

    Love and Care supports children who are unable to go to school due to financial or personal challenges. Through our Brightening Futures Through Education program, we provide school supplies, books, and basic learning materials to help children continue their education.

    We also encourage students who have dropped out to return to school by creating awareness and offering support where possible. Our aim is to make education more accessible for children in rural and low-income communities.

    Read More

  • Empowering Women Through Education and Support

    Empowering Women Through Education and Support is our mission to help girls and women reclaim their right to learn, grow, and lead. We provide adult education, skill development, and bridge courses for female students who dropped out early due to social or economic constraints.

    We also run awareness programs on women’s health, legal rights, and self-development. By encouraging education and economic independence, we aim to build a society where women are confident, skilled, and empowered to shape their future.

    Read More

  • Caring Hands for the Differently-Abled

    Our Caring Hands for the Differently-Abled program supports individuals with physical and mental disabilities through education, therapy, and rehabilitation services. We aim to integrate them into society by offering customised learning tools, vocational training, and life skills support.

    With a compassionate approach, we strive to create equal opportunities for the visually impaired, hearing and speech challenged, and mentally disabled individuals. Our goal is to ensure that every person, regardless of ability, can live a life of dignity and inclusion.

    Read More

  • Compassionate Homes for the Elderly

    The Compassionate Homes for the Elderly initiative provides a safe, loving, and respectful environment for senior citizens. We offer residential care through our old age homes where elders receive medical attention, nutritious food, and emotional support.

    Many elderly people are left alone or neglected. Our program ensures they are not just cared for, but also engaged in social, spiritual, and recreational activities. We believe every senior deserves to age gracefully, surrounded by dignity and warmth.
