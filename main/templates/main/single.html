Social Welfare and Relief Activities.

The Love and Care Social Club is committed to social welfare and humanitarian relief, focusing on the care and protection of society’s most vulnerable groups. The organization aims to provide safe shelter, nourishment, and emotional support through the establishment of hostels and care homes for orphans, destitute women, the elderly, persons with disabilities, and other marginalized individuals. In times of natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, and pandemics, the club actively engages in emergency relief and rehabilitation efforts by distributing food, clothing, medicine, and other essential supplies. Furthermore, it undertakes a range of charitable initiatives including community kitchens, food drives, and livelihood support programs to uplift economically disadvantaged families, ensuring their basic needs are met with compassion and dignity..
