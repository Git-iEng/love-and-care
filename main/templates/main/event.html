Opening Ceremony – 8th March 2025

The Love and Care Foundation was officially launched on 8th March 2025, a date that coincides with International Women’s Day — a global celebration of strength, compassion, and empowerment. It was the perfect day to begin a journey rooted in empathy, service, and positive impact. This memorable event brought together our founding team, dedicated supporters, and well-wishers to mark the beginning of our mission to serve humanity with purpose.

The ceremony commenced with a traditional ribbon-cutting moment, symbolising the opening of doors to a mission filled with empathy and hope. The occasion also served as a powerful reminder of the role women play in nurturing communities and leading social change — many of our team members and supporters being incredible women driven by a passion to uplift others.

Attendees came together to reflect, connect, and share personal motivations behind joining this noble cause. We discussed upcoming initiatives, long-term goals, and reaffirmed our shared vision to build a world where no one feels forgotten.

Photos were taken to capture the joy and energy of the day — smiling faces, hopeful hearts, and the unmistakable excitement of launching something truly meaningful. The energy of the event was not just celebratory; it was a collective promise to stand by those in need and make a real difference.

This Women’s Day-inspired opening ceremony set the tone for everything that lies ahead. With renewed motivation and unity, the Love and Care Foundation moves forward to create lasting change and empower lives with compassion.
